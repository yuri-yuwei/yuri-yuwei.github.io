Scientific focus

科学问题

How can remotely sensed spectral and structural metrics be used to characterize plant biodiversity, scale biodiversity mapping from field plots to landscapes and satellites, and evaluate biodiversity–ecosystem function relationships under changing climate conditions?

如何利用遥感获取的光谱和结构指标刻画植物多样性，将生物多样性监测从样地扩展到景观和卫星尺度，并进一步评估气候变化背景下生物多样性与生态系统功能之间的关系？

Approach

研究方法

I combine NEON field observations, airborne hyperspectral imagery, LiDAR/TLS point clouds, satellite observations, biodiversity metrics, and statistical modeling.

我结合 NEON 地面观测、机载高光谱影像、LiDAR/TLS 点云、卫星观测、生物多样性指标和统计建模方法开展研究。

Research themes

主要研究主题

My research uses remote sensing to measure biodiversity as a multidimensional ecological property, linking spectral variation, forest structure, functional traits, ecosystem function, and satellite-based geospatial positioning across scales.

我的研究利用遥感方法刻画多维度生物多样性，并将光谱变化、森林结构、功能性状、生态系统功能以及星载遥感定位方法联系起来。

Theme 01

Biodiversity Remote Sensing

生物多样性遥感

I examine how imaging spectroscopy and spectral diversity metrics capture taxonomic, phylogenetic, and functional dimensions of plant biodiversity across NEON biomes, and how climate and vegetation context mediate the spectral diversity–biodiversity relationship.

我研究成像光谱和光谱多样性指标如何刻画植物分类、系统发育和功能多样性，并分析气候和植被类型如何影响光谱多样性—生物多样性关系。

Theme 02

LiDAR and Forest Structure

LiDAR 与森林结构

I use terrestrial laser scanning and airborne LiDAR to quantify individual-tree and stand-level canopy structure, including tree segmentation, crown architecture, canopy gaps, and structural heterogeneity, with Duke Forest as an important local testbed.

我利用 TLS 和机载 LiDAR 量化单木和林分尺度的冠层结构，包括单木分割、树冠结构、林窗和结构异质性，并以 Duke Forest 作为重要研究样地。

Read more Project description
Theme 03

Biodiversity–Ecosystem Function Relationship

生物多样性—生态系统功能关系

I test whether remotely sensed foliar functional traits and derived functional diversity metrics can explain spatial variation in ecosystem functions, including gross primary production, leaf area index, and coarse woody debris across forest biomes.

我研究遥感反演的叶片功能性状和功能多样性指标是否能够解释生态系统功能的空间差异，包括 GPP、LAI 和粗木质残体等指标。

Theme 04

Spaceborne LiDAR & Photogrammetry

星载 LiDAR 与摄影测量

I developed methods for extracting ICESat-2/ATLAS spaceborne LiDAR control points and constructing a global elevation control point database to support satellite stereo-image block adjustment and improve elevation positioning accuracy without dense ground control.

我研究 ICESat-2/ATLAS 星载激光控制点提取和全球高程控制点数据库构建方法，用于辅助光学卫星立体影像区域网平差，并提升少控制或无地面控制条件下的高程定位精度。
