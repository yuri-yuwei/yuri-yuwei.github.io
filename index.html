Environmental remote sensing
环境遥感 · 森林生态 · 生物多样性

Yu Wei

I work at the intersection of forest biodiversity, imaging spectroscopy, LiDAR, and ecosystem function. My research connects spaceborne and airborne remote sensing with ecological questions across scales.

我的研究关注森林生物多样性、成像光谱、LiDAR 与生态系统功能之间的关系，尝试将星载/机载遥感数据与生态学问题连接起来。

Current focus

当前关注方向

Remote sensing indicators of plant biodiversity, canopy structure, phenology, and ecosystem function across forest ecosystems.

利用遥感指标研究森林生态系统中的植物多样性、冠层结构、物候变化与生态系统功能。

Methods

Hyperspectral imagery, LiDAR/TLS, ecological inventories, biodiversity metrics, and statistical modeling.

高光谱影像、LiDAR/TLS、生态调查数据、生物多样性指标与统计建模。
