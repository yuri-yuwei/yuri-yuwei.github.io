Research themes

研究方向

My research develops remote sensing approaches for monitoring forest biodiversity, canopy structure, and ecosystem function across spatial scales. I combine field observations, airborne imaging spectroscopy, LiDAR/TLS, satellite products, biodiversity metrics, and statistical or AI-based modeling to ask how biodiversity can be measured from above and how it responds to environmental gradients and global change.

我的研究关注如何利用遥感方法跨尺度监测森林生物多样性、冠层结构与生态系统功能。我结合地面生态观测、机载成像光谱、LiDAR/TLS、卫星产品、生物多样性指标以及统计和 AI 建模方法，探索如何从遥感数据中刻画生物多样性，并理解其对环境梯度和全球变化的响应。

A central goal of my work is to link ground-based ecological observations with airborne and satellite remote sensing, so that biodiversity can be monitored from individual trees and field plots to landscapes, biomes, and eventually continental scales.

我的研究主线是将地面生态观测与机载、星载遥感数据连接起来，使生物多样性监测能够从单木和样地尺度扩展到景观、生物群系乃至大陆尺度。

Theme 01

Biodiversity Remote Sensing

生物多样性遥感

This research direction asks whether spectral information from imaging spectroscopy can serve as a reliable proxy for plant biodiversity. I focus on the spectral diversity–biodiversity relationship across ecological gradients, with particular attention to taxonomic, phylogenetic, and functional dimensions of biodiversity. A key question is why this relationship is strong in some biomes but weak or non-positive in others, and how climate, vegetation type, and evolutionary history mediate remote sensing signals.

这一方向关注成像光谱中的光谱信息是否能够作为反演植物生物多样性的可靠指标。我重点研究光谱多样性—生物多样性关系在不同生态梯度中的表现，尤其关注分类多样性、系统发育多样性和功能多样性。核心问题是：为什么这一关系在某些生物群系中较强，而在另一些生物群系中较弱甚至不显著？气候、植被类型和进化历史如何影响遥感信号与生物多样性之间的关系？

  • Imaging spectroscopy
  • Spectral diversity
  • Phylogenetic diversity

12/2023 – 08/2024

Spectral Diversity and Plant Biodiversity across NEON Biomes

NEON 生物群系中的光谱多样性与植物多样性关系

Integrated NEON field sampling data with airborne hyperspectral imagery to evaluate how spectral diversity metrics relate to plant biodiversity across broad biogeographic regions. The project examined whether phylogenetic diversity can be better captured by hyperspectral remote sensing than taxonomic diversity and how environmental factors alter the spectral diversity–biodiversity relationship.

该项目将 NEON 地面植物调查数据与机载高光谱影像相结合，评估光谱多样性指标在大尺度生物地理梯度中与植物多样性的关系。研究重点包括：高光谱遥感是否更适合捕捉植物的系统发育多样性（相对于分类多样性而言），以及环境因子如何改变光谱多样性—生物多样性之间的相关关系。

08/2023 – 10/2024

Forest Regeneration Sampling Fieldwork

森林再生与植物生物多样性野外调查

Collected field data on seedling density, sapling growth, and seed production to support forest regeneration research in Pennsylvania. This field experience provides ecological grounding for remote sensing analyses by connecting canopy-scale observations with regeneration processes below the canopy.

该项目在宾夕法尼亚州开展森林再生野外调查，主要采集幼苗密度、幼树生长速度和种子数量等数据。项目为遥感分析提供了重要的地面数据，使冠层尺度的遥感观测能够与森林再生过程建立联系。

Theme 02

LiDAR and Forest Structure

LiDAR 与森林结构

This direction focuses on measuring three-dimensional forest structure using airborne LiDAR and terrestrial laser scanning. I am interested in how tree height, crown size, canopy gaps, vertical complexity, and stand-level structural heterogeneity shape biodiversity patterns and provide structural habitat information that cannot be captured by optical imagery alone.

这一方向关注如何利用机载 LiDAR 和地基激光扫描刻画森林三维结构。我关注树高、树冠大小、林窗、垂直结构复杂性和林分尺度结构异质性如何影响生物多样性格局，以及这些结构信息如何补充传统光学影像难以直接捕捉的生态信息。

  • LiDAR
  • TLS
  • Canopy structure
  • Tree segmentation
  • Duke Forest
09/2024 – 04/2025

Mapping Biodiversity in Duke Forest with Advanced Remote Sensing

利用先进遥感技术开展 Duke Forest 生物多样性制图

Designed a Duke Forest research project using terrestrial laser scanning to characterize forest biodiversity and structure. The project involves field data collection with the RIEGL VZ-400i scanner, LiDAR point cloud processing, individual tree segmentation, and analysis of crown architecture, canopy gaps, and forest structural heterogeneity.

该项目以 Duke Forest 为研究样地，利用地基激光扫描刻画森林生物多样性与结构。项目包括使用 RIEGL VZ-400i 进行野外扫描、处理 LiDAR 点云、进行单木分割，并分析树冠结构、林窗和森林结构异质性。

基于 AI 与遥感的森林生物多样性制图

Led a Duke Data+/Climate+ student project that combines hyperspectral imagery, airborne LiDAR, and deep learning to improve tree species identification and forest biodiversity mapping. 12/2023 – 07/2024

Forest Biodiversity Modeling with Hyperspectral, LiDAR, and Tree Inventory Data

融合高光谱、LiDAR 与树木调查数据的森林生物多样性建模

Used hyperspectral remote sensing data, LiDAR-derived structural information, and tree inventory data to study the relationship between plant biodiversity and remotely sensed spectral or structural metrics. This work also included individual tree crown delineation from dense forest canopies and species-level classification using hyperspectral imagery.

该项目利用高光谱遥感数据、LiDAR 提取的结构信息和树木调查数据，研究植物多样性与遥感光谱/结构指标之间的关系。项目还包括在密集森林冠层中进行单木树冠分割，并基于高光谱影像进行单木尺度的树种分类。

Theme 03

Biodiversity–Ecosystem Function Relationship

生物多样性—生态系统功能关系

This research direction examines whether remotely sensed functional diversity can explain spatial variation in ecosystem functions. I focus on functional traits retrieved from imaging spectroscopy and ask how functional richness, evenness, and divergence relate to productivity, canopy development, and decomposition-related indicators across biomes.

这一方向关注遥感反演的功能多样性是否能够解释生态系统功能的空间差异。我重点研究成像光谱反演的叶片功能性状，并分析功能丰富度、功能均匀度和功能分异度如何与生产力、冠层发育和分解相关指标相联系。

  • Functional diversity
  • Foliar traits
  • GPP

05/2024 – 12/2024

Functional Diversity as a Predictor of Ecosystem Functions

功能多样性作为生态系统功能预测指标

Derived multidimensional functional diversity from remotely sensed foliar trait maps across NEON terrestrial sites and evaluated its relationship with ecosystem function indicators, including gross primary production, leaf area index, and coarse woody debris. The results suggest that functional richness can serve as a useful proxy for ecosystem productivity, while the strength and direction of biodiversity–ecosystem function relationships vary across biomes.

该项目基于遥感反演的叶片功能性状图，在 NEON 站点尺度上计算多维功能多样性，并评估其与生态系统功能指标之间的关系，包括总初级生产力、叶面积指数和粗木质残体。结果表明，功能丰富度可以作为生态系统生产力的有效代理指标，但生物多样性—生态系统功能关系的强度和方向在不同生物群系中存在差异。

Theme 04

Spaceborne LiDAR & Photogrammetry

星载 LiDAR 与摄影测量

My earlier work at Wuhan University focused on geometric positioning in photogrammetry and remote sensing. I developed and evaluated methods for extracting ICESat-2/ATLAS spaceborne laser control points, constructing global elevation reference databases, and integrating spaceborne LiDAR with optical satellite stereo imagery to improve block-adjustment positioning, especially under limited or no ground-control conditions.

我在武汉大学阶段的研究集中于摄影测量与遥感几何定位。我开发并评估了 ICESat-2/ATLAS 星载激光控制点提取方法、全球高程参考数据库构建方法，以及将星载 LiDAR 与光学卫星立体影像结合以提升区域网平差定位精度的方法，尤其关注少控制或无地面控制条件下的高程定位精度提升。

  • ICESat-2/ATLAS

  • Photogrammetry
  • Block adjustment

11/2022 – 05/2023

Master’s Thesis: Global Spaceborne LiDAR Control Points and Block-adjustment Positioning

硕士论文：全球星载激光控制点数据库构建及辅助区域网平差定位

Developed a methodological framework for extracting ICESat-2/ATLAS laser control points, constructing a global control point database, and applying laser control points to optical satellite stereo-image block adjustment. The work addressed practical challenges in large-scale control point extraction, association between laser points and optical imagery, and accuracy evaluation without dense ground control.

硕士论文建立了 ICESat-2/ATLAS 激光控制点提取、全球控制点数据库构建以及激光控制点辅助光学卫星立体影像区域网平差的方法框架。研究解决了大范围激光控制点提取、激光点与光学影像关联、以及缺少密集地面控制点条件下精度评价等问题。

03/2022 – 10/2022

Stereo Photogrammetry Integrating High-resolution Satellite Imagery and Spaceborne LiDAR

融合高分辨率卫星影像与星载 LiDAR 的立体摄影测量应用

Synthesized optical satellite imagery and satellite laser altimetry data to derive high-resolution elevation products and support natural resource management applications. The project explored how spaceborne laser control points can improve the geometric accuracy of optical stereo imagery and support downstream environmental analyses.

该项目融合光学卫星影像和星载激光测高数据，生成高分辨率高程产品，并服务于自然资源管理应用。研究重点是星载激光控制点如何提升光学立体影像的几何定位精度，并为后续环境分析提供基础数据。

Global Spaceborne LiDAR Point Cloud Database Development

全球星载 LiDAR 点云数据库功能开发

Downloaded, analyzed, filtered, and organized spaceborne LiDAR data, particularly ICESat-2/ATLAS, to support the construction of a global elevation reference database. This work provided the data foundation for extracting reliable laser control points at large spatial scales.

该项目负责下载、分析、筛选和组织星载 LiDAR 数据，尤其是 ICESat-2/ATLAS 数据，用于构建全球高程参考数据库。该工作为大范围提取可靠激光控制点提供了数据基础。
