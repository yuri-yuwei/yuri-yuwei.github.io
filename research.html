Research themes

研究方向

My research connects geospatial data, remote sensing, and ecological theory to understand how forests are structured, how biodiversity varies, and how ecosystem functions can be monitored from above.

我的研究连接空间数据、遥感技术与生态学问题，关注森林结构如何形成、生物多样性如何变化，以及生态系统功能如何通过遥感进行监测。

1. Biodiversity remote sensing

1. 生物多样性遥感

This line of work examines whether and how imaging spectroscopy can capture biodiversity patterns across ecological gradients. It focuses on the relationship between spectral diversity and biological diversity, including species richness, phylogenetic diversity, and functional diversity.

这一方向关注成像光谱是否能够、以及如何捕捉不同生态梯度下的生物多样性格局。核心问题包括光谱多样性与物种丰富度、系统发育多样性、功能多样性之间的关系。

  • NEON
  • Imaging spectroscopy
  • Spectral diversity
  • Phylogenetic diversity
  • Functional diversity

2. Forest structure, phenology, and ecosystem function

2. 森林结构、物候与生态系统功能

This research theme uses LiDAR, terrestrial laser scanning, and remotely sensed ecological indicators to study canopy structure, phenology, productivity, and ecosystem function across forest sites.

这一方向利用 LiDAR、地基激光扫描和遥感生态指标，研究森林冠层结构、物候变化、生产力与生态系统功能之间的关系。

  • LiDAR
  • TLS
  • Canopy structure
  • Phenology
  • GPP / LAI

3. Spaceborne LiDAR and photogrammetric positioning

3. 星载 LiDAR 与摄影测量定位

Earlier work at Wuhan University focused on ICESat-2/ATLAS laser altimetry, global control point extraction, and the integration of satellite stereo imagery with laser control points for geometric positioning.

武汉大学阶段的研究主要围绕 ICESat-2/ATLAS 激光测高数据、全球高程控制点提取，以及利用星载激光控制点辅助卫星立体影像几何定位。

  • ICESat-2/ATLAS
  • Control points
  • Photogrammetry
  • Block adjustment
  • Geospatial positioning

Selected projects

代表性项目

Spectral biodiversity across NEON sites

NEON 站点尺度的光谱—生物多样性关系

Using airborne imaging spectroscopy and ecological inventories to evaluate how spectral metrics relate to plant biodiversity across U.S. biomes.

利用机载成像光谱与生态调查数据，评估光谱指标如何反映美国不同生物群系中的植物多样性。

AI-driven mapping of forest biodiversity

基于 AI 的森林生物多样性制图

A Duke Data+/Climate+ project on using machine learning and remote sensing to map forest biodiversity.

Duke Data+/Climate+ 项目，探索利用机器学习与遥感数据进行森林生物多样性制图。

Project page

Duke Forest canopy structure and turnover

Duke Forest 冠层结构与动态变化

Using terrestrial laser scanning to characterize canopy architecture, gap structure, and branch/leaf turnover.

利用地基激光扫描刻画森林冠层结构、林隙结构以及枝叶周转过程。

Global ICESat-2 laser control point database

全球 ICESat-2 激光控制点数据库

Constructing and evaluating global elevation control points from ICESat-2/ATLAS data for geospatial positioning.

从 ICESat-2/ATLAS 数据中构建和评估全球高程控制点，用于空间定位与区域网平差。
